What is an IT Helpdesk?

An IT helpdesk is a service that provides technical support to users within an organization. It acts as the first point of contact for employees or customers who experience technical issues with hardware, software, or network systems.

Main Functions of a Helpdesk

  • Handle user support requests
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Managing service tickets
  • Providing system guidance

Helpdesk vs Technical Support

Helpdesk Technical Support
First point of contact Handles complex issues
Basic troubleshooting Advanced problem solving
